Financial Analyst

Cellnex Portugal Lisbon, Portugal (Hybrid)
Full time

About the job

Direct reporting to Accounting & Tax within local Finance direction/area/department.

Assist the company in accounting, tax and treasury roles, ensuring the coordination of activities between different parties providing services within this roles and ensuring the proper financial registers, the statutory and legal obligations and contributing for the overall development of projects and transversal activities accordingly with the goals set.


MAIN FUNCTIONS:
  • Ensure the interface between the external accounting company, the shared services center/ BPO and the Corporate/ consolidation areas, preparing all the necessary information, monitoring the work developed and ensuring the deliverables in a proper and timely manner
  • Support of Purchase to Pay (PTP), Order to Cash (OTC) and Record to Report (RTR) activities
  • Access the accounting system, extract data and ensure compliance with deadlines and speed up all accounting processes (in IFRS and in the local accounting system)
CANDIDATE PROFILE:

Academic profile: Degree/ Bachelor degree in Accounting, Finance, Management or Economics

Languages: English and Portuguese (proficient) - mandatory; Spanish (Basic) - desirable

Experience: At least 5 years experience in finance and accounting areas.
