Project Manager

Cellnex Portugal Lisbon, Portugal (Hybrid)
Full time

About the job

Inserted in the Management Systems Department, the selected candidate will be part of a team with extensive experience in the telecommunications and infrastructure industry and will have the opportunity to develop projects in different Cellnex PT departments.

The Management System Department defines and implements processes to ensure that the company is operating effectively, and suggests improvements to the company's operations, integrated management systems (IMS) and processes, contributing to the quality of the assets detained and the services provided by Cellnex Portugal, and contributing to the achievement of the company's operational, environmental and OH&S goals.


MAIN FUNCTIONS:
  • Support the planning, coordination and control of operations, maintenance and construction and assist with the definition of quality assurance policies and procedures.
  • Participate in operational processes monitoring and compliance.
  • Support the development, implementation and control of business improvement processes to ensure the services are delivered with quality and on time.
CANDIDATE PROFILE:

Academic profile: Performance of excellence in a leading university. Bachelor / Master Degree in Engineering, Economics, Management or Finance

Languages: English (Proficient; Mandatory); Portuguese (Intermediate; Desirable); Spanish (Basic; Desirable)

Experience: > 2 years of previous experience in consultancy, project management, business process improvement or in similar functions.
